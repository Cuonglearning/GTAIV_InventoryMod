# GTAIV Inventory Mod
An advanced GTA IV mod featuring food and inventory management system with survival mechanics.

## Features

### 🍖 Advanced Food & Inventory System
- **Dynamic Inventory Management**: Carry and manage food items with customizable capacity limits
- **NPC Looting**: Loot items from NPCs with a comprehensive loot detection system
- **Item Database**: Extensive item catalog with food, valuables, scraps, and collectibles
- **Multi-page Menu System**: Organized UI for browsing items across multiple categories:
  - Food & Drinks (soft drinks, coffee, snacks, meals)
  - Valuables (gold, jewelry, watches, electronics)
  - Scraps (metal scrap and collectibles)
- **Shop System**: Buy and sell items with shopping cart functionality
- **Core System**: 
  - Health Core: Regenerates over time based on inventory
  - Deadeye Core: Special tactical gameplay mechanic
  - Gold Core: Unlock unique abilities
- **Item Combining**: Craft and combine items from your inventory
- **Backpack System**: Upgrade carrying capacity with different backpack types
- **Survival Mechanics**: Realistic health regeneration tied to food consumption
